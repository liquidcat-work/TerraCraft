Terracraft v0.7 “Survival Craft” · Three.js voxel engine

How to Play

  • WASD — move
  • Mouse — look around
  • Space — jump
  • Shift — sprint
  • 1-9 — select hotbar slot
  • Left click — mine a block
  • Right click — place a block / open furnace & crafting table
  • E — inventory
  • F — eat food (survival)
  • Esc — pause

Survival Mode

  • Punch trees → get logs → craft planks → craft sticks
  • Make a crafting table (4 planks) for 3×3 crafting
  • Make a wooden pickaxe to mine stone
  • Make a furnace (8 cobblestone) to smelt ores
  • Mine iron ore with a stone pickaxe → smelt into iron ingots
  • Craft an iron pickaxe to mine diamond ore
  • Watch your hunger — eat food to stay alive!
